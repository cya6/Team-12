Introduction

Welcome to The Onions' website documenting Team 12's progress in creating our robot that can map out a maze!

Check out our labs and milestones to see how we first started off to completing our finished robot!
Not only can it map out a maze, it can also:

  • Follow a line
  • Detect and avoid crashing into other robots
  • Detect a 660Hz starting tone
  • Transmit the maze information to an on-screen GUI
